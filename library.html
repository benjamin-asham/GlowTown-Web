Service

Delivery

he library’s delivery services ensure that patrons can access resources conveniently without visiting the library in person. Users can request books, magazines, or other materials through the library’s online portal, and have them delivered directly to their homes or workplaces. This service is especially beneficial for individuals with mobility challenges, busy schedules, or those living in remote areas.

Quiet and Comfort

The library is dedicated to providing a calm and peaceful environment for its visitors. Designed with comfort in mind, the library offers quiet reading areas, private study rooms, and cozy seating arrangements to ensure an ideal atmosphere for concentration and relaxation. The spaces are well-lit, air-conditioned, and equipped with minimal distractions, making them perfect for reading, studying, or working.

Lending Books

The library offers a comprehensive range of borrowing and book-related services to meet the needs of its visitors. Patrons can borrow books for a specified period and enjoy the convenience of an online catalog to search for available titles. Additionally, the library provides a reservation system that allows users to place holds on books currently checked out.

Digital Service

The library offers a variety of digital services to enhance accessibility and convenience for its users. These include an extensive e-library where visitors can access eBooks, audiobooks, and digital magazines. The library also provides online research tools, such as databases and academic journals, to support students and researchers. Virtual assistance is available through live chat or email for inquiries and support.
